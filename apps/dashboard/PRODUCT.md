# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Product, engineering, support, and growth teams investigating what happened in a real customer session. They need to move quickly from a session list to the exact click, error, or friction point without losing context.

## Product Purpose

Orange Replay records privacy-safe browser sessions, lets teams watch them live or replay them later, and surfaces errors and rage clicks so a problem can be understood and fixed quickly.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, and trustworthy. The interface should feel focused and technical without becoming cold, noisy, or theatrical.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic analytics dashboards, oversized decorative cards, heavy gradients, weak gray-on-gray contrast, cramped data rows, browser-default focus treatments, and controls that look interactive without working.

## Design Principles

- Chrome whispers; session evidence speaks.
- Make the next diagnostic action obvious.
- Keep dense data readable through spacing and alignment.
- Use color and shape together for status and friction.
- Preserve keyboard access with deliberate, quiet focus states.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast. Every action must be keyboard reachable with a visible focus state. Do not rely on color alone, respect reduced motion, and keep dense replay data legible at common desktop and narrow-screen sizes.
