More than a replay button. Live watch, an instant timeline, privacy by default, and one-command self-host — all in the same edge-native box.

Live now

Live isn't a delayed batch job bolted on the side — the session's Durable Object is already a WebSocket hub, so following a user in real time (and jumping in when a checkout stalls) costs almost nothing. Join mid-session and a checkpoint keyframe catches you up in seconds.

Instant timeline

The scrubber paints from the manifest before a single byte of video-DOM downloads — errors and rage clicks are already plotted. Jump to the moment it broke, then start loading.

Self-host in minutes

One wrangler deploy to your own Cloudflare account. No ClickHouse, no Kafka, no Kubernetes — recordings never leave your infrastructure.

Privacy on by default

Inputs, text, and PII are masked in the browser before capture — default-deny, not an opt-in checkbox. The server never even receives the raw bytes.

Residency as a checkbox

Pin a project's recordings to a region — Durable Object and R2 jurisdictions, a real guarantee, not a placement hint. Metadata elsewhere is scrubbed.

A player built for people who live in the replay

Step frames and skip to errors from the keyboard, change speed, skip idle time, and scale any recording to fit your viewport. Cursor trails, click ripples, and rage bursts are drawn right on the replay.

Deep-link any session

getSessionUrl() drops a replay link straight into Sentry, Datadog, or your own logs — jump from a stack trace to the exact session that threw it.

One session, every team. Product, engineering, support, and growth all open the same replay — each from their own angle.
